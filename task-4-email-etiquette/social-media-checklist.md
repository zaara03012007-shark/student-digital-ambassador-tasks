Do's (✅):
 1. Maintain a professional profile on LinkedIn.
 2. Fact-check information before sharing a post.
 3. Respect the privacy of your peers and professors.
 4. Use clear, concise language in professional groups.
 5. Credit the original creator when sharing content.

Don'ts (❌):
 1. Post sensitive or private information about yourself or others.
 2. Use unprofessional or aggressive language in comments.
 3. Engage in "cyber-loafing" or spamming during group work.
 4. Share "fake news" or unverified rumors.
 5. Post content that could negatively impact your future employment.
